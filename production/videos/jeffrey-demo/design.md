## Overview
Dark tech demo for Jeffrey, the Evolve agentic system. Futuristic but precise — Data Drift energy with Deconstructed sharpness. 

## Colors
Deep near-black blue background (#080810). Electric cyan (#00d4ff) as primary accent. Violet (#7c3aed) for orchestration. Green (#22c55e) for execution. Amber (#f59e0b) for skills. Never flat black, always blue-tinted dark.

## Typography
Space Grotesk 800 for display headlines. Space Grotesk 300 for body/subheads. JetBrains Mono for labels, code, terminal content. Pairing tension: geometric sans for authority, monospace for precision.

## Do's and Don'ts
- DO use radial glows at 15-25% opacity behind focal elements
- DO use hairline rules (2px) in accent colors to separate zones
- DO keep backgrounds alive with slow breathing ambient scale
- DON'T use gradient text (background-clip) — it's an AI tell
- DON'T use identical card sizes in the agent grid
- DON'T add drop shadows — depth comes from glow and z-layering
